TeeSea Community Tools

I built these tools as a way to easily customize, stylize, and extract data from your careers portal. They are open-source, totally free, and ready to be used by the wider university community.

Open Source Hub

Core Portal Builders

🛠️

Custom Pane Maker

Generate responsive, dynamic HTML banners for your portal. Easily segment who sees what using Apache Velocity logic and user tags—no coding required.

Launch Maker
📆

Event Calendar Widget Maker

Create a branded monthly calendar view for your students. Paste your RSS feed URL and customize the colors to generate an interactive, live-synced calendar widget.

Launch Maker
✏️

Event Description HTML Builder

Ensure your event descriptions always look perfectly formatted. This tool turns basic text inputs into highly styled, responsive HTML ready to paste directly into the system.

Launch Maker
📅

Event RSS Reader

Turn your Events RSS feed into a sleek, responsive card widget. Choose between a horizontal carousel, a grid, or a vertical list layout perfect for VLE integration.

Launch Maker
💼

Vacancies RSS Reader

Turn your Vacancies RSS feed into a sleek, responsive job board widget. Features dynamic deadline tracking, employer icons, and customizable layouts.

Launch Maker

Other Useful Tools

📱

QR Code Maker

It's notoriously difficult to find a free QR generator that doesn't force you to create an account. This tool lets you instantly generate and download transparent, high-res QR codes for your posters—100% free and private.

Launch Maker
🪟

Iframe Generator (3rd Party)

A brilliant third-party tool for generating clean iframe embed codes. Super useful for quickly embedding external web pages, forms, or multimedia resources directly into Pathway activities and custom panes.
